Dungeon Soundboard

Cinematic sound for your tabletop RPG sessions.

View on App Store

Description

Elevate your tabletop RPG sessions from simple dice rolls to unforgettable, immersive experiences. Dungeon Soundboard is the ultimate audio companion for Game Masters, allowing you to seamlessly weave cinematic soundscapes using Spotify for ambiance and trigger custom sound effects for critical moments. Whether it's the roar of a dragon, the clang of swords, or the eerie silence of a dungeon, you have complete control over the auditory world of your game.

Key Features

  • Spotify Integration: Instantly access and play ambient music and soundscapes from any Spotify playlist to set the perfect mood.
  • Custom Soundboard: Upload and organize your own sound effects for quick access during gameplay. Perfect for monster roars, spell effects, and character actions.
  • Intuitive Interface: Designed for GMs in the heat of the moment, the interface is clean, simple, and allows for quick triggering of sounds without disrupting the game's flow.
  • Crossfade & Looping: Smoothly transition between ambient tracks and loop background noises for continuous atmosphere.
