Privacy Policy for DJ BPM Assistant

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

DJ BPM Assistant does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. The app operates entirely on-device. The only data saved is your app preferences (BPM values, appearance settings, and feature toggles), stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. No account or login is required to use the app.

2. In-App Purchases

Subscription payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Subscription status is managed by Apple's StoreKit framework and verified locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

DJ BPM Assistant does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

4. Data Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no user data at risk. All app state is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS's built-in security model.

5. Children's Privacy

DJ BPM Assistant does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
